Lasagna

Description

Enjoy this straightfoward and easy lasagna recipe created by my mother. Start by cooking vegetables with garlic and onion, then add tomato sauce and simmer. Cook the noodles until just tender, and in a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with some grated Parmesan. In a baking dish, layer the meat sauce, noodles ricotta mixture then top with shredded mozzeralla. Repeat the layers, ending with a final layer of mozerella. Bake in the oven until cheese is bubbly and golden brown and serve warm.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 Capsicum
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 1 Yellow Pepper
  • 1 leaf of Basil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar of tomato sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozerella cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Pre heat Oven: Preheat your oven to about 375 degrees F
  2. Ready Vegetables:
    • Use a chopping board to cut the capsicum, bell pepper, and yellow pepper
    • In a large skillet, stir fry the cut veggies until browned
    • Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook until softened
    • Stir in the tomato sauce and season with salt and pepper, letting it simmer for 10 minutes
  3. Cook Noodles: While the sauce simmers, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  4. Prepare Ricotta Mixture: In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg and grated Parmesan cheese. Season with a little salt and pepper.
  5. Assemble Lasagna:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded mozzarella over the ricotta.
    • Add another layer of meat sauce.
    • Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, meat sauce).
    • Finish with a final layer of noodles, remaining meat sauce, and top with the last cup of shredded mozzarella.
  6. Bake: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Serve: Let the lasgna rest for ten minues before slicing. Serve each slice warm.
