Chicken Alfredo


Description

Savor the rich and creamy flavors of Chicken Alfredo, a classic Italian-American dish that's perfect for any occasion. This recipe combines tender, juicy chicken breasts with a luxurious Alfredo sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese. Tossed with fettuccine pasta, this dish is easy to make and incredibly satisfying. The creamy sauce clings to the pasta, while the chicken adds a delicious, savory element. Garnish with fresh parsley for a touch of color and freshness. Perfect for a comforting weeknight dinner or a special meal, this Chicken Alfredo will become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook Chicken:
    • Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
    • In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
    • Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked and golden brown.
    • Remove the chicken from the skillet, let it rest for a few minutes, then slice it into thin strips.
  2. Cook Pasta:
    • While the chicken is cooking, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    • Add the fettuccine pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  3. Make Alfredo Sauce:
    • In the same skillet used to cook the chicken, melt the butter over medium heat.
    • Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute, until fragrant.
    • Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    • Gradually stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
    • Season the sauce with salt and black pepper.
  4. Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet with the alfredo sauce and toss the pasta in the sauce until it's wellc oated. Added the sliecd chicken to the skillet and gently mix it in with the pasta and the sauce
  5. Serve: Garnish with chopped fresh parsely and serve warm,
